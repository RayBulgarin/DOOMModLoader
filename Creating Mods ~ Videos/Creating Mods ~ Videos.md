Mods can include custom videos. Videos must be placed within "*video/mods/*" (or a nested subfolder) in your mod, and must be in the Bink *.bik* or *.bk2* formats.\
[**[The RAD Video Tools]**](https://www.radgametools.com/bnkdown.htm) can convert videos to *.bik* with the "*Bink it!*" > "*Bink*" button.

Mods cannot replace the vanilla video files, but you can edit the corresponding "*generated/decls/material/\*.decl*" and "*generated/decls/mapinfo/\*.decl*" [**[game resources]**](Creating-Mods) to point to your custom videos instead.

> [!NOTE]
> Video file paths/names may only contain lowercase a-z, 0-9, "_", "/", and the "*.bik*" or "*.bk2*" extension.
